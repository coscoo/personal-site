# Hello, World

Welcome to my little corner of the internet.

I've been meaning to start writing publicly for a while. This is that start.

## Why Write?

Writing forces clarity. When I try to explain something in prose, I find out quickly whether I actually understand it or just think I do. It's the best debugger for fuzzy thinking.

## What to Expect

I'll write about things I'm building, ideas I'm exploring, and lessons I've learned the hard way. No particular cadence — just when I have something worth saying.

Thanks for reading.
